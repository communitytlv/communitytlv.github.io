Join us on April 29, 2025 at AWS Floor 28 for the <span style="color: #faa344"><a href="https://lu.ma/aws-devops-2025" target="_blank">DevOps X AWS Startup Circuit event</a></span>, and on December 11th at Expo Tel Aviv for our larger summit.</p>
